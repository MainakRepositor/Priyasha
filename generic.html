About Me

A few lines about the person behind Priyasha.

Hi there, I am Priyasha Jha. I am a law student from West Bengal. Art is my passion and creativy is my nerve-tongue. I love to visualize the beauty of the world using my paint brushes. I love to do things in a creative, bohemian way and so is the definition of me.

I have started a startup to deliver art as a gift to my clients at a reasonable rate. Ranging from sketches to water-colour I have an expertise over various forms of visual arts and graphics.
